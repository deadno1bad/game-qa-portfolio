# Bug Report

## ID
> BUG_004

---

## Title
>[Collision][Level] Gap between terrain meshes creates collision hole

---

## Environment

**Game:**
>Drive Nomad Demo

**Version / Build:**
> v1.3

**Platform:**
>Steam

**Operating System:**
>Windows 11

**Game Mode:**
>Singleplayer

**Language:**
> Russian

**PC Specifications (optional):**
CPU:I5-13400f
GPU:RTX 2060
RAM:16

---

## Preconditions

> Player has access to the affected map area.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Launch the game.
2. Go to the affected location.
3. Move the character toward the gap between the terrain meshes.
4. Observe the terrain and collision.

---

## Expected Result

>Terrain meshes should be seamlessly connected.
>Collision should completely block access through the terrain.
>The player should not be able to see or interact through gaps.


---

## Actual Result

>A visible gap exists between terrain meshes.
>Collision is also broken in the same location, allowing the player to partially move into the gap and see through the terrain.

---

## Reproducibility

> Always (100%)

---


## Severity

>Medium

---

## Priority

>Medium

>Collision / Level Design
