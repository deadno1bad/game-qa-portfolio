# BUG-001

## Title

**[UI][Localization][Inventory] Large Plant 1 icon flickers in the Crafting Bench when Russian localization is selected**

---

## Game

Solarpunk

---

## Version

v1.0.5-301061

---

## Platform

PC

---

## Environment

- Windows 11
- Russian Localization
- Solo and Multiplayer

---

## Preconditions

- Game language is set to **Russian**.
- Large Plant 1 has been unlocked.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Launch the game.
2. Change the game language to **Russian**.
3. Start a Solo or Multiplayer session.
4. Unlock **Large Plant 1**.
5. Open the **Crafting Bench**.
6. Locate **Large Plant 1** in the crafting menu.

---

## Expected Result

Large Plant 1 should be displayed correctly with a stable icon in the Crafting Bench.

---

## Actual Result

The Large Plant 1 icon rapidly flickers between two different images, causing incorrect visual representation in the Crafting Bench.

---

## Reproduction Rate

5/5 (Always)

---

## Severity

**Minor**

Visual issue only. Does not affect gameplay but negatively impacts the user interface.

---

## Priority

**Medium**

Should be fixed to improve UI quality before future updates.
